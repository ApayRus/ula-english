insomniac – someone who can’t sleep easily

to get sucked into something – not being able to stop yourself getting involved with something that you don’t want to do

sleep deprived – not having enough sleep

resist the urge – to stop yourself acting on a strong feeling to do something

feel-good factor – doing something that gives someone a happy and positive feeling

regret – sad feeling you get when you’ve made a mistake and wished you hadn’t made the mistake in the first place
