congested – overcrowded

utopia – an imaginary place where everything is perfect

smog – air pollution

urban sprawl – the way a city spreads into undeveloped land around it, often without planning permission

migrate – move from one place to another

shanty towns – poor communities where the houses are built out of cheap materials like corrugated iron and plastic sheeting

off the grid – without facilities such as an electricity or water supply

infrastructure – the basic facilities a town or city needs, for example: communication, transport, water, and electricity

ubiquitous – everywhere

game changer – significantly affecting the result of something
