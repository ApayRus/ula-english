sedentary – adjective used to describe a lifestyle which involves a lot of sitting and not much exercise

life expectancy – the age to which you are expected to live

shirker – someone who avoids doing something they don’t like, usually because they are lazy

tongue-in-cheek – something meant to be humorous and not taken seriously

stern – adjective that means serious and strict

clipboard – a board you attach papers to so that you can write on them as you walk around
