fashion victim – someone who always wears what’s thought to be fashionable, even if it doesn’t actually look good on them

on-trend – in keeping up with the latest fashion

eye-catching – attracting attention and being noticed

traction – if something gains traction it becomes accepted and popular

understated – (fashion) something that does not attract attention and is not that impressive

thorny (adjective) – a thorny issue is a subject that is difficult to deal with and discuss
