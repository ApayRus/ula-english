LED light – electronic device that lights up when electricity is passed through it - abbreviation of ‘light emitting diode’

wavelength – the distance between two waves of light which makes things appear to us in the different colours of the rainbow

photon – single unit of light

microgravity – weaker pull of gravity in space, making things float and seem weightless

plasticity – ability of a plant to adapt to and cope with changes in the environment by changing its biological structure

genes – chemicals and DNA in the cells of plants and animals which control their development and behaviour
