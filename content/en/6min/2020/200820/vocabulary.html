airborne – carried  and spread in the air

metropolis – largest, busiest and most important city in a country or region.

antibiotics – chemical or medicine, like penicillin, that can destroy harmful bacteria or stop their growth

spread like wildlife – (idiom) spread quickly around many people

commute – distance you travel on your journey between your home and your place of work

livelihood – job or other way of earning money to pay for what you need to live
