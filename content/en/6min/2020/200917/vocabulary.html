scepticism – attitude of doubting whether something is true or useful

resistance – refusal to accept a change or new idea

apprehensive – feeling worried that something bad is going to happen

do away with (something) – to remove it completely or put an end to it, (phrasal verb)

custodian – someone with responsibility for taking care of something or trying to protect ideas or principles

stigma – strong feeling or shame or disapproval which most members of a community have towards something
