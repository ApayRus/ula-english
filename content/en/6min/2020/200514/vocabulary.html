frequency – how often something repeats; electrical current frequency is the number of times a wave repeats a positive-to-negative cycle

synchronous – occurring together at the same time, place or rate

a bit of a pickle – informal expression meaning a difficult situation with no obvious answer

charge up – get the power needed to work; recharge

anomalies – differences from what is usual or expected

solid state – electronics using semiconductors which have no moving parts and can automatically convert different electrical currents
