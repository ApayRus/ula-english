great apes – group of large primates including chimpanzees, gorillas and orang-utans; they have no tail and are humans’ closest animal relatives

modifying – changing something slightly in order to improve it

tool – instrument or simple piece of equipment, for example a knife or hammer, that you hold in your hands and use for a particular kind of work

gestures – movements made with parts of the body like hands, arms or head, to express ideas and feelings

pat – touch someone or something gently and repeatedly with your hand held flat

alleviate – reduce the intensity or severity of someone’s pain or suffering
