pond scum – a slang name for microalgae like spirulina and chlorella

having a moment – becoming more well-known and popular at this moment in time; similar to ‘trending’

superfood – a type of food which is especially full of healthy nutrients like vitamins, minerals and protein

becoming stretched – not enough resources to meet people’s needs

sustainable intensification – food produced in ways which don’t damage the environment or use new land

organism – any plant or animal life form
