mindset – someone’s way of thinking and the general attitudes and opinions they have about something; mentality

intelligence – secret information about a person, government or country

cover story – false story someone tells in order to hide the truth

data breach – occasion when private information can be seen by people who should not be able to see it

malware – computer software and viruses that are designed to damage the way a computer works

infiltrate – secretly enter a group or organisation in order to spy on it or influence it
