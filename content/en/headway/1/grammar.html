1.5. Plural nouns

1. Most nouns add -s in the plural. stamps keys cameras 2. If the ends in -s, -ss, -sh, or -ch, add -es.
bus buses
class classes
wish wishes
match matches
3. If the noun ends in a consonant + -y, the y changes to -ies.
country countries
party parties
But if the noun ends in a vowel + -y, the -y, the -y doesn't change.
key keys
day days
4. Some nouns are irregular. Dictionaries show this.
child children
person people
woman women
man men
